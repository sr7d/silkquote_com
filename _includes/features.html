How It Works

Our streamlined 3-step process transforms Salesforce opportunities into professional quotes in minutes:

1) Build Templates

Build dynamic templates with conditional fields, custom sections, and flexible layouts. Configure pricing rules, product bundles, and business logic to create professional quotes that adapt to your business needs.

2) Generate Quotes

Automatically generate professional PDF quotes from Salesforce opportunities. Preview quotes before saving, apply draft watermarks, and include signature blocks. Streamline your quote generation process with intelligent automation.

3) Share Documents

Manage your quote templates with ease. Set paper sizes, configure logos, attach terms and conditions, and create reusable templates. Share quotes via email or generate shareable links for seamless collaboration.
