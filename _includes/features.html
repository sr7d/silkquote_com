How It Works

Our simple 3-step process takes you from template setup to sharing in minutes:

1) Build Templates

Admins configure flexible templates directly in Salesforce with dynamic fields, conditional sections, and standardized terms & conditions all without code.

2) Generate Quotes

Reps generate polished PDFs with one click pulling in products, pricing, and customer information automatically, so every quote is accurate and on-brand.

3) Share Documents

Quotes can be previewed, saved to Salesforce, or shared via email, secure link, or integrated e-signature keeping the sales cycle moving fast.
