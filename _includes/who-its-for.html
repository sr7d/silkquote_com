Is SilkQuote Right for You?

SilkQuote is for you if:

  • You need professional, branded PDF quotes from Salesforce
  • Your Process Is:
    Opportunity → Quote PDF → E-Sign → Order & Invoice
  • You want to be live in days, not months
  • You value simplicity, automation, and high user adoption

SilkQuote is NOT a fit if:

  • You need complex product configuration (bundles, constraints, guided selling)
  • You require advanced pricing engines
  • You're looking for a full CPQ suite with billing and subscription management
