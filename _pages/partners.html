For Salesforce Consultants & SI's

Add a free quoting system to every Salesforce engagement and charge for the setup.

SilkQuote is a free, AppExchange-listed Salesforce quoting app. Your clients get a production-ready quote workflow. You get a repeatable services package.

How you make money

The app is free for your clients. The setup, customization, and ongoing support are yours to scope and bill. Here's what that typically looks like:

  • Template design & build Custom-branded PDF layouts, conditional sections, product tables. $1,500–$5,000
  • Automation & flow setup Auto-generate quotes from Opportunity stages, trigger approvals, route to deal room. $2,500–$8,000
  • CPQ migration assist Help clients off Salesforce CPQ (end-of-sale) or Conga onto a lighter quoting layer. $5,000+
  • Ongoing retainer Template updates, new product lines, admin support. $500–$2,000/mo

What delivery looks like

The Quickstart package is a defined scope you can drop into any proposal. Install, configure, and launch SilkQuote for your client's Salesforce org. They leave with live quoting, a branded template, and reps who know how to use it.

Suggested scope: App install, up to 2 branded PDF templates, Opportunity field mapping, deal room setup, quote generation flow or button, rep training, and admin handoff documentation.
