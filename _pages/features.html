Template Design

  • On Brand Design: Upload a logo and set an accent color per template.
  • Drag-and-Drop Editor: Reorder fields in each PDF section.
  • Conditional Field Display: Show or hide fields and columns based on record values.
  • Conditional Terms: Terms and Conditions with conditional availability.
  • Smart Template Selection: Filters by Opportunity field values automatically.
  • Line Item Grouping: Group line items by any field with subtotals.

Quote Generation

  • One-Click PDF Generation: PDF quote from any Opportunity via Quick Action.
  • Flow Invocable Action: Trigger quote generation from any Salesforce Flow.
  • In-Browser Preview: Review the PDF before saving or sending.
  • Live Quote Preview: See the current quote in a live panel directly from the Opportunity.
  • Stale Quote Indicator: Flag PDFs that are out of date with the current Opportunity data.
  • Quote Version History: Retain all past quote versions per Opportunity.

Sharing & Acceptance

  • Email Quote Links: Send prospects a direct link to their quote from the Opportunity.
  • Prospect Acceptance: Prospects accept or decline quotes directly, no account needed.
  • Email Open Tracking: Know when a prospect opens your quote email.
  • Activity Tracking: Log every quote action: sent, viewed, accepted, declined.

Pricing

  • Product Catalog Browser: Browse and search all active pricebook products directly from the Opportunity.
  • Field-Set Filters: Filter the catalog with dropdowns and search—fields driven by a Product2 field set, no code required.
  • Dynamic Display Columns: Show any Product2 fields as columns in the product list via a field set.
  • Product Bundles: Define bundle relationships once; child products auto-expand when a bundle is added to the quote.

Admin & Reporting

  • Approval Routing Compatible: Build your approval processes around template selection.
  • Configuration Validator: Scan templates, dynamic fields, and terms for problems.
  • Dashboard & Reporting: Analyze opportunities, templates, and activity to spot success patterns.
